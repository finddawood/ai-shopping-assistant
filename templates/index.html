🧠 How the AI Works

A transparent look at the recommendation engine powering ShopAI

01
📝

Product Tokenisation

Every product's name, description, category, and tags are broken into tokens. A shared vocabulary of 677 unique terms is built across all 40 products.

tokens = name_words + desc_words + tags + [category]
02
📊

TF-IDF Vectorisation

Each product becomes a numerical vector. TF rewards products rich in relevant terms. IDF penalises common words that appear everywhere.

TF-IDF(t,d) = TF(t,d) × log((N+1)/(df(t)+1)) + 1
03
🎯

Cosine Similarity

Your preferences are converted to the same vector space. Similarity is the cosine angle between your preference vector and each product vector.

sim(A,B) = (A·B) / (‖A‖ × ‖B‖)
04
⚡

Hybrid Scoring

Raw similarity is blended with quality signals. Deals get a 15% uplift ensuring high-relevance AND high-quality products rank at the top.

score = sim×0.6 + (rating/5)×0.4 × (1.15 if deal)
05
🧩

Session Learning

Every time you add a product to your cart, its tags are appended to your preference profile. Future recommendations improve automatically.

session["liked_tags"] += product.tags
06
💡

Budget Optimisation

A greedy algorithm sorts all affordable candidates by value score and selects items until your budget is exhausted. Runs in O(n log n).

value = (rating × (1 + discount)) / price
